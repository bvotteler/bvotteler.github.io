About

I’ve spent over 20 years of my career creating, designing, and building software.

Over that time, I’ve accumulated heaps of experience integrating systems. Whether that we integrated systems via chatbots, app links, push- or pull-based APIs & webhooks, or even good old-fashioned nightly batch jobs.
There are several ways to go about making systems talk to each other, and I’ve seen many variations thereof.

My company

I founded Bespoke Systems Integration Pty Ltd with the purpose of building useful products/plugins/addons for various ecosystems.
Truth be told, at the time of writing this (Mar 2021), I haven’t narrowed down my focus onto specific platform(s) just yet. There are plenty of possibilities, and I have more research to do before picking the most promising ones. And discarding others that do not work well for solo builders like me.

Another goal, or “arm” of the business (for lack of a better term) is providing consulting services for clients' custom integrations. I may even end up building those where it makes sense.
I want to share my years of experience by helping clients create a map that shows the path from an idea to a finished product in clear, distinct steps. How and when to measure what, and how to reduce risk and costs along the way.

Motivation

After all these years, I still love working with code. I like to understand the business and/or customer problems the software is supposed to solve, build a clear understanding of what success should look like, and find ways to measure progress sooner rather than later during the lifetime of a project.

The perfect project for me is having unambiguous metrics in place to be able to track progress towards a clear goal which benefits a business and its customers.
