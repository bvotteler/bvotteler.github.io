About

I have spent over 20 years of my career creating, designing, and building software.

A lot of the projects I found I had a natural inclination of doing well in had to do with integrating various systems. Making them share data with each other, or process information from another system into a useful, new form.
I suspect my natural inclination to reduce duplicate information and rather work directly with the “source of truth” to keep any data consistent - except for rare exceptions - plays a big part in that.

Tech

Much of my recent development work centered around Java, Node, and the AWS stack (e.g. lambdas, SQS, Dynamo et al.) on the backend. I know enough React to be considered dangerous to the front-end.
Apart from those, I have worked in a lot of other languages. If you are looking for something specific, my LinkedIn profile should be able to provide a more complete list as part of my employment history.

Projects I have worked on range from overnight batch jobs for banks, building content moderation tools, all the way to putting together chatbots to interface with various systems. Many of which centered around the idea of giving systems access to data of other ones.

Motivation

After all these years, I still love working with code. I believe this is driven by my desire to understand the business problems we are trying to solve, having a clear idea of what success looks like, and actively measuring progress sooner rather than later in the life of a project.
Part of that includes being in regular contact with stakeholders and clients. Feedback is an important part of building software that matters. Feedback & concerns, together with success measures help me understand if we need to reconsider our approach, pivot or, in extreme cases, accept when measures tell us a piece of work may not prove valuable enough to pursue it any further.

Location

I am based in northern NSW, Australia. However, I have no reservations of working with any location. In fact, I have had successful projects with clients as far away as Europe.
