About

I’ve spent over 20 years of my career creating, designing, and building software.

Naturally, I’ve accumulated heaps of experience integrating systems. Whether that we integrated systems via chatbots, app links, push- or pull-based APIs & webhooks, or even good old-fashioned nightly batch jobs.
There are several ways to go about making systems talk to each other, and I’ve seen many variations thereof.

My company

I founded Bespoke Systems Integration Pty Ltd with the purpose of building useful products/plugins/addons for various ecosystems.
My aim is to build up a portfolio of small products rather than setting out with the goal to build one, big product. Some smaller projects may evolve eventually. So, never say “never”.

My secondary goal is providing consulting services for clients' custom integrations. I may even end up building those where it makes sense.
I want to use my past experience to help potential clients create a map that shows the path from an idea to a finished product in clear, distinct steps. How and when to measure what, and how to reduce risks and costs along the way.

Motivation

After all these years, I still love working with code. I like to understand the business and/or customer problems the software is supposed to solve, build a clear understanding of what success should look like, and find ways to measure progress sooner rather than later during the lifetime of a project.

The perfect project for me is having unambiguous metrics in place to be able to track progress towards a clear goal which benefits a business and its customers.
